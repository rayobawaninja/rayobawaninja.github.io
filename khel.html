KHEL

An app and NGO that looks forward to revolutionize the sports industry in Pakistan

Khel, an innovative NGO and mobile app, was founded by Raiyaan Bawany and Hashir Kashif, two visionary A-level students from Nixor College, with a mission to revolutionize sports dynamics and accessibility in Pakistan.

Collaborating with Karachi United, Khel has spearheaded initiatives to enhance football accessibility for the underprivileged, bridging gaps and fostering inclusivity in sports across communities in Pakistan. Through their joint efforts, they have empowered countless individuals to engage in the beautiful game, enriching lives and nurturing talent at grassroots levels.
