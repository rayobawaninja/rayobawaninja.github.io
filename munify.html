MUNIFY

An app designed to aid students in Model United Nations conferences

MUNIFY is a mobile application developed by me to assist students participating in Model United Nations (MUN) conferences. The app utilizes a blend of Artificial Intelligence Systems (AIS) to provide students with comprehensive research tools and help them find suitable debate topics.

With MUNIFY, students can access a wide range of resources, including historical data, academic papers, and expert opinions, to enhance their understanding of international relations and current affairs. The app's intuitive interface and personalized recommendations streamline the research process, allowing students to focus more on honing their debate skills and crafting compelling arguments.
