MADSCALP NINJA BY RAIYAAN

MADSCALP NINJA by Raiyaan is a NinjaTrader strategy designed to make trading smoother. It's coded in C# and helps traders spot important moments. When break lines cross, it gives colored signals and plays sounds, making decision-making faster.

This tool was made for John Ondercin, one of the biggest crypto trader, by Raiyaan Bawany, blending advanced tech with market expertise to boost trading success
